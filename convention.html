Why Groups Choose TallBoy

We've been the after-hours spot for conferences, trade shows, and corporate events at the Convention Center since 2019.

No Rental Fee

We don't charge to use the space. Bring your team of 10 or your department of 50. Just let us know you're coming and we'll make sure you're taken care of.

A One Minute Walk

Late Night Kitchen

Your dinner meeting ran late. The networking reception had no food. We're still serving smoked wings, smash burgers, and fries til close. Every night.

Award-Winning Beer

RAMMY Finalist for Beer Program of the Year, two years running. Taps rotate daily. Happy hour Mon-Fri 4-6pm with $3 off all beers, $6 house margaritas, and $6 wine. Your team will be impressed without you blowing the company card.

Happy Hour + Daily Deals

Happy hour Mon-Fri 4-6pm, plus a different deal every night of the week. Wing discounts, burger and beer combos, bingo, trivia. Time your post-session gathering right and your group gets a great deal.

Casual, Not Corporate
