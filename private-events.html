Your Event at TallBoy

Whether it's a birthday bash, group hang, or a casual after-work takeover, TallBoy is the move. We don't charge a rental fee. Just bring your people, we'll handle the rest.

What We Offer

Full bar service with our RAMMY-nominated beer program. Kitchen open late. TVs for watch parties. A space that feels like home, not a conference room.

Event Types

Birthday parties, watch parties, rehearsal dinners, holiday parties, launch events, a Tuesday night takeover because you feel like it, team outings, or corporate events.

Convention Center Groups

Attending a conference at the Walter E. Washington Convention Center? We're around the corner with our own entrance on 9th Street. Group dinners, post-session drinks, exhibitor parties. A one minute walk and you're here.
