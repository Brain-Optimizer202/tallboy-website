About TallBoy

Built by a bartender, for everyone.

The Story

TallBoy opened in 2019 in Shaw, Washington DC. Built by bartenders who wanted somewhere local, approachable, and actually fun to go after a shift. Somewhere you could unwind without breaking the bank, in a cool atmosphere with great beer, great cocktails, and zero attitude. The people who built it are still behind it every day.

The name comes from the 16oz tall cans that are the bar's signature. Nothing about TallBoy is complicated. Sit at the bar and chat with your bartender. Grab a stool outside and enjoy the sunshine. Watch the game. Meet your next friend. It's that kind of place.

It's where your bartender goes after bartending somewhere else. It's where your neighbor watches the game. It's where your friend's birthday accidentally turns into a late night situation. The kitchen stays open til close, which is rare in Shaw. When everywhere else has shut down, TallBoy is still serving food.

Busy night at TallBoy

The Space

Tin ceiling, dim lighting, and a room that feels like it's been lived in. Plenty of outdoor seating when the weather's right. No dress code, no attitude, no velvet rope. Just a comfortable spot where you can actually hear the person next to you.

There are TVs for watching games. There's a bar you can sit at and actually talk to the person making your drink. The energy is whatever you need it to be: afternoon wind down, evening hangs with friends, late night food run when everywhere else has closed. We're also part of the Convention Center building with our own entrance on 9th Street, so there's always a mix of neighbors and visitors from whatever's happening next door.
