What We're Known For

Cold beer, smoked wings, and a neighborhood bar built by a bartender, for everyone.

Smoked Wings

Slow smoked, perfectly crisped, tossed in your choice of sauce. Classic Buffalo, Memphis Dry Rub, Old Bay, Lemon Pepper, or General Tso. The reason people drive across the city.

RAMMY-Nominated Beer

Finalist for Beer Program of the Year, two years running. Taps rotate daily with fresh picks from breweries across the country. $3 off all beers during happy hour.

Late Night Kitchen

When everywhere else in Shaw has closed, we're still serving food. Smash burgers, tacos, wings, grilled cheese. Kitchen stays open til close.

The Vibe

Your home away from home. The place you go to get away from a busy day, sit at the bar, and actually have a conversation with your bartender. There's a game on the TV if you want it. Good music on if you don't. The energy is whatever you need it to be: afternoon wind down, evening hangs with your crew, late night food run when everywhere else has closed. Pull up a stool and stay a while.
